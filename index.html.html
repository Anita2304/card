TURKEY

Turkey is a country straddling Eastern Europe and Western Asia, with cultural ties to ancient Greece, the Persian Empire, the ancient Roman Empire, the Byzantine Empire, and the Ottoman Empire. Cosmopolitan Istanbul on the Bosphorus Strait is home to the city's iconic Hagia Sophia Museum, with soaring domes and Christian mosaics. Other attractions in the city include the magnificent 17th-century Blue Mosque and Topkapi Palace, built around 1460 and once the seat of the Sultan. Ankara is the modern capital of Turkey.

THAILAND

Thailand is a Southeast Asian country known for its tropical beaches, gorgeous royal palaces, ancient ruins, and ornate temples decorated with Buddha statues. Bangkok, the capital, is a city of modern urban spaces side by side with quiet canal-side communities and famous temples such as Wat Arun, Wat Pho and the Temple of the Emerald Buddha (Wat Phra Kaew). Nearby beach resorts include bustling Pattaya and fashionable Hua Hin.Capital : Bangkok Currency : Baht

SOUTH KOREA

South Korea is an East Asian country located in the southern half of the Korean Peninsula, with a very tight military demarcation line with North Korea. South Korea is also known for its verdant rolling hills dotted with cherry blossom trees and centuries-old Buddhist temples, seaside fishing villages, subtropical islands and high-tech cities like its capital, Seoul.
